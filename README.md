# 🛒 Blinkit Grocery Sales Dashboard

## 📌 Short Description
An interactive and visually engaging Power BI dashboard built to analyze Blinkit grocery sales performance across outlet types, item categories, outlet sizes, and customer ratings. The dashboard provides actionable insights into sales trends, product distribution, and outlet performance to support better business decisions in the quick-commerce and grocery retail industry.

## 🛠 Tech Stack
* **Power BI Desktop** – Main platform used for creating interactive reports and visualizations
* **Power Query** – Data cleaning, transformation, and preprocessing
* **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Used for calculated measures and KPIs
* **Excel / CSV** – Source data preprocessing and formatting
* **SQL** – Data extraction and analysis queries
* **File Formats:** `.pbix` for development and `.png` for dashboard previews
* 
## 📂 Data Source
**Source:** Blinkit Grocery Dataset(Kaggle)
**Dataset Includes:**
* Item type and fat content
* Outlet establishment year
* Outlet size and location tier
* Sales amount and item visibility
* Customer ratings and item count
The dataset helps analyze grocery sales performance across different outlet categories and customer preferences.

## ✨ Features / Highlights
### 📊 Business Problem
Quick-commerce platforms like Blinkit manage thousands of grocery products across multiple outlets. Without proper analytics, it becomes difficult to monitor sales performance, customer preferences, and outlet efficiency.

## 🎯 Goal of the Dashboard
To build an interactive analytics dashboard that:
* Tracks overall sales and average sales performance
* Monitors item distribution and customer ratings
* Analyzes outlet establishment trends over time
* Compares sales across outlet sizes and locations
* Identifies top-performing item categories and outlet types

# 📈 Walkthrough of Key Visuals
## 🔹 KPI Cards (Top Section)
Displays overall business performance metrics:
* **Total Sales:** $1.20M
* **Average Sales:** $141
* **Number of Items:** 8,523
* **Average Rating:** 3.92

## 🔹 Outlet Establishment Trend (Line Chart)
Shows the growth of outlet establishments over the years and highlights peak-performing years in terms of sales contribution.
**Insight:**
2018 recorded the highest outlet sales contribution at approximately **$205K**.

## 🔹 Fat Content Analysis (Donut + Bar Charts)
Analyzes sales based on item fat content categories:
* Low Fat
* Regular
**Insight:**
Regular fat products contribute slightly higher total sales compared to low-fat products.

## 🔹 Item Type Performance (Horizontal Bar Chart)
Displays sales contribution across various grocery categories such as:
* Fruits & Vegetables
* Snack Foods
* Household
* Dairy Products
* Frozen Foods
**Insight:**
Fruits & Vegetables and Snack Foods are among the top-performing categories.

## 🔹 Outlet Size Analysis (Donut Chart)
Compares sales distribution across outlet sizes:
* Small
* Medium
* High
**Insight:**
Medium-sized outlets contribute the highest overall sales.

## 🔹 Outlet Location Analysis (Bar Chart)
Visualizes sales performance based on outlet location tiers.
* Tier 3 outlets generate the highest sales
* Tier 1 outlets show comparatively lower sales contribution

## 🔹 Outlet Type Table
Detailed comparison of outlet types using metrics like:
* Total Sales
* Number of Items
* Average Sales
* Average Rating
* Item Visibility
**Insight:**
Supermarket Type1 contributes the highest total sales and item count among all outlet types.

# 📌 Business Impact & Insights
### ✅ Sales Optimization
Identify high-performing product categories and outlet types to improve inventory planning.
### ✅ Customer Preference Analysis
Understand demand for regular vs low-fat products and optimize product offerings.
### ✅ Location-Based Strategy
Tier 3 locations show stronger performance, helping businesses focus expansion strategies.
### ✅ Outlet Performance Monitoring
Track underperforming outlets and improve operational efficiency.
